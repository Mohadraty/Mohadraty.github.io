Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Mohadraty ("App") owned and operated by Omar Farouk. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App on web, Android, and iOS platforms.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, student ID, and other identifying details.
  • Location Data: GPS and network-based location for attendance verification.
  • Biometric Data: Fingerprint authentication on supported devices.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, and browser details.
  • QR Code Data: Information scanned for attendance marking.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To verify student attendance using QR codes, location fencing, and biometrics.
  • To improve and personalize user experience.
  • To maintain security and prevent fraudulent attendance marking.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. However, we may share data with:

  • Service providers assisting in app functionality and analytics.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. User Rights

  • Request a copy of your stored data.
